About Scour

Scour is a website made in regards to a project for the Cloud Computing Subject as Software as a Service and Web Development 1. This is to be deployed on a internet hosting service. The hosting service I used is Heroku. This site will allow you to explore a wide variety of artist powered by the Spotify API and listen to a certain preview track.

Scour is a school project and requirement, made in the hopes of passing the subjects and is made with the help of MaterializeCSS.

Warning: Once submitting please wait awhile as it is depending on your current internet speed.

Creator

Isaiah Evans A. Villa Abrille is a third year Software Engineering Student from the University of the Immaculate Conception.

Steps
  • Step 1

    Enter the Artist/Band's Name.

  • Step 2

    Click on the Submit Button.

  • Step 3

    Scroll down to find the player or click on the Preview on the Top Right Side of the site.

  • Step 4

    Confirm the artist by playing a specific preview of them powered by the Spotify API.
